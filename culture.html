Exploring the World Around Us

Cultural studies in Montessori help young children connect with the world and understand their place in it. At Maya Montessori, we introduce geography, science, music, and world traditions through hands-on exploration, maps, nature work, and stories. These activities build a sense of wonder and respect for people, places, and life all around us.

What We Focus On

Geography Basics: Children explore land and water, continents, and maps using tactile puzzle pieces and globes.
Seasons & Weather: Learning how our world changes through the yearâ€”tracking the seasons, clouds, and temperature.
Cultural Awareness: We talk about customs, foods, languages, and music from around the world to build empathy and interest.
Practical Life Connections: Celebrating holidays, exploring flags, or learning to say greetings in different languages.

Cultural Sequence

  • Land & Water: Identifying oceans, lakes, islands, and continents
  • Continents & Countries: Exploring world maps, puzzle globes, and cultural models
  • Seasons & Weather: Learning about the four seasons, climate, and simple weather patterns
  • People & Places: Exploring traditions, foods, music, and languages from around the world
  • Flags & Landmarks: Matching flags and learning about famous places in each continent
  • Spanish Language: Daily exposure to greetings, numbers, songs, and commands to build familiarity with Spanish
