What Makes Maya Montessori Special

At Maya Montessori, we believe exceptional care is about more than just routines—it’s about connection, trust, and purpose-driven learning. Here's what truly sets us apart.

Real Transparency

You never have to wonder what your child is doing—you can see it. With live camera access and an open-door policy, you're always welcome and always in the loop.

True Partnership

From your first tour to each daily hand-off, we’re here to support you. We communicate with intention, listen with care, and work with you to make transitions smooth and meaningful for your child—and for you.

Intimate, Home-Based Environment

We intentionally keep our group small so every child gets true one-on-one attention. We take time to understand your child’s unique rhythm, needs, and joys—and we meet them where they are.

Thoughtful Value for Families

We aim to offer better value than most daycares in the area—with no surprise fees, free meals and supplies for 6 months, and support for potty training included in your rate.

Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At Maya Montessori, our work goes far beyond academics. We believe in nurturing the whole child—mind, body, and spirit—through meaningful hands-on learning and culturally rich experiences. Children are encouraged to explore the world through interactive lessons, creative play, and self-directed discovery.

Our Montessori curriculum emphasizes respect for diversity, independence, and the natural curiosity every child brings into the classroom. Whether we’re planting seeds in the garden, learning about world cultures, or exploring through sensory play, every moment is designed to foster confidence, empathy, and a lifelong love of learning.

A Peek Into Our Montessori Day

Our classroom hums with purposeful activity—from sweeping and pouring to painting, gardening, and storytelling. Children explore at their own pace, choosing materials that spark curiosity and build skills across all areas of development. Every moment is an opportunity to grow with independence, joy, and wonder.

Practical Life

Daily tasks that build independence, coordination, and confidence.

Sensorial Exploration

Refining the senses through exploration of color, texture, and sound.

Math

Building number sense and patterns with tactile, hands-on materials.

Language

Developing speech, sound, and expression through story and song.

Science

Encouraging curiosity through observation, experimentation, and nature.

Culture

Exploring geography, traditions, and languages from around the world.
