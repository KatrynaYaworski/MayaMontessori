Program Options

Our programs are thoughtfully designed for children ages 18 months to 5 years, offering consistency, flexibility, and deep Montessori engagement. Whether you're easing into care or need full-day support, every child receives the same high-quality experience.

Full-Time Care

Hours: 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM (up to 10 hours)

Tuition: $1,200/month

Add-Ons: Meals ($80), Camera Access ($40)

Includes full Montessori curriculum, outdoor time, nap/rest, and one-on-one guidance.

Part-Time Care

Options: 2 or 3 days per week

Tuition: $700–850/month

Same enriching structure as full-time, with added flexibility for family needs.

What's Included

  • Certified Montessori instruction
  • Potty training support
  • Small group setting (max 12 children)
  • All materials and supplies included
  • Daily developmental updates
  • Optional catered meals and camera access
