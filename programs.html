Milestones & Age Groups

From first steps to first sentences, Maya Montessori supports the full spectrum of early childhood development.

Infant 0–18 Months
  • Rolling, crawling, standing, walking
  • Grasping and manipulating objects
  • Exploring with senses
  • Early communication: babbling & gestures
  • Secure caregiver bonds
    • Toddler 18 Months–3 Years
  • Running, climbing, fine motor skills
  • Sorting, naming, following directions
  • Simple sentences & emotional expression
  • Parallel play & independence
    • Preschool 3–5 Years
  • Basic math & patterning
  • Fine motor (cutting, drawing, writing)
  • Friendships, empathy & cooperation
  • Creative play, music, and cultural exploration

    • Maya Montessori currently welcomes children ages 2 to 5. Programs for younger and older age groups may be introduced in the future.
