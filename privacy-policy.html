Privacy Policy

At Maya Montessori, your family’s privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, and your child’s name when you:

  • Fill out a form on our website
  • Contact us via email or the contact page
  • Schedule a tour or request information

We do not use tracking cookies. If this ever changes, we will update this policy before implementing them.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule visits
  • To communicate important updates or changes
  • To improve the content and functionality of our website

Sharing Your Information

We never sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers only when necessary to operate our website or communicate with you, and only under confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information if required to do so by law.

Cookies

Our site does not currently use cookies. If cookie functionality is added in the future to enhance your experience, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security. By using our site, you acknowledge this risk and agree to use the site at your own discretion.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page along with the new effective date.

Contact Us
